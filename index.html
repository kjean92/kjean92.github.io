About Me

Prior to learning software engineering, I worked for several years as an Emergency Medical Services dispatcher. With long, irregular hours and frequent exposure to high-stress & traumatic situations, burnout and PTSD are common amongst emergency services workers. I hope to use the skills I'm learning through Thinkful to create a product that makes self-care and mental health resources accessible to those that need it (in a fun, gamified package).
